Experience

I started at home but as I started to enjoy baking more I enrolled in culinary school at the Art Institue, after a year there I was able to get a job decorating cakes at a bakery in Riverisde, CA and rose through the ranks until I was presented with a better oppurtunity at Bottega Louie. At Bottega I was able to learn how to make a much larger variety of pastries. All the while I continued to take on side projects and have completed thousands of cakes, you can take a look below at some of my favorite works.
